About I-Care

We are building a future where preventive eye care is simple and universal

I-Care combines applied AI, clean design, and healthcare-first thinking to help people detect risk earlier and act with confidence.

Our Story

Why we started

Eye health is often ignored until symptoms become severe. Many people face preventable vision problems simply because early tools are unavailable, expensive, or hard to use.

I-Care was created to close that gap through a frictionless digital experience. Students, professionals, and older adults can all access meaningful eye screening from one platform.

By pairing AI analysis with practical guidance, we help users move from passive awareness to proactive habits that protect long-term vision.
