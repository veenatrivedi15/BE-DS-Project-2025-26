🍼 Early Childhood Assessment (16-30 months)

👁️ Social Engagement & Eye Contact

Does the child make eye contact when you call their name?
Does the child point to show you interesting things?
Does the child show interest in other children?

🗣️ Communication & Language

How does the child respond when you call their name?
How does the child communicate their needs?

🎭 Play & Imitation

Does the child engage in pretend play (feeding dolls, pretend phone calls)?
Does the child imitate your actions (clapping, waving)?

🔄 Repetitive Behaviors & Sensory

Does the child have repetitive movements (hand flapping, spinning, rocking)?
How does the child react to changes in routine or environment?
Does the child have unusual reactions to sounds, textures, or lights?

🎨 Preschool Assessment (2-5 years)

👥 Social Communication

Does the child engage in back-and-forth conversation?
Does the child show interest in playing with other children?
Does the child share enjoyment or achievements with others?

🎭 Imaginative Play & Creativity

Does the child engage in complex pretend play with storylines?
How does the child typically play with toys?

🔄 Behavioral Patterns & Interests

Does the child have intense, narrow interests?
Does the child understand and use nonverbal communication?
How does the child respond to sensory experiences?
How does the child handle routine changes?
Does the child show empathy when others are upset?

📚 School Age Assessment (6-12 years)

🏫 Social Functioning

How does the child interact with peers at school?
Does the child understand social rules and expectations?
How does the child handle group conversations?

🎯 Interests & Academic Functioning

Does the child have very specific, intense interests?
How does the child handle changes in schedule or routine?

🎭 Emotional Understanding & Communication

Does the child understand others' emotions and perspectives?
How does the child understand humor, sarcasm, or figurative language?
Do sensory sensitivities affect the child's daily activities?
How independent is the child with age-appropriate tasks?
How does the child handle conflicts or disagreements?

🎓 Adolescent Assessment (13-18 years)

👥 Social Relationships & Communication

How does the adolescent maintain friendships?
Does the adolescent understand complex social situations?
How does the adolescent handle romantic relationships or dating?

🎯 Interests & Independence

Does the adolescent have specialized interests that dominate their time?
How independent is the adolescent with daily living skills?

🧠 Emotional & Cognitive Patterns

How does the adolescent handle stress and emotional regulation?
Does the adolescent understand abstract concepts and complex language?
Do sensory preferences significantly affect daily choices?
How does the adolescent handle group activities or team situations?
How does the adolescent plan for the future (college, career, independence)?
